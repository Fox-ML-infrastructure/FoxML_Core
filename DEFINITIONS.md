# Definitions (Binding)

**"LGBTQ+ protections"**  
Means explicit, written, enforceable nondiscrimination policies and support programs protecting 
lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and gender-diverse people against discrimination, harassment, 
or institutional harm.

**"Material benefit"**  
Means tangible financial, operational, or strategic advantage, including but not limited to forecasting, 
budgeting, optimization, modeling, planning, or resource allocation.

**"Public-interest mission"**  
Means healthcare, medical research, patient care, community services, and public-health infrastructure.

**"Prohibited financial activity"**  
Includes but is not limited to:
- proprietary trading
- alpha research
- quantitative modeling for profit
- hedge-fund strategies
- market-making
- risk-arbitrage
- speculative financial activity
- strategic allocation benefiting investors, shareholders, or profit-seeking groups

**"Commercial actor"**  
Any for-profit organization, firm, business unit, or department whose primary purpose is generating revenue.

**"Interpretive loophole"**  
Any attempt to reword, reframe, or strategically reinterpret clauses to bypass the intent of this license.
All such attempts are invalid.
