# Institutional Compliance Checklist

Any institution relying on the Additional Permission in EXCEPTION.md must satisfy ALL criteria:

### A. LGBTQ+ Protection Requirements
- [ ] The institution maintains written, enforceable nondiscrimination protections for LGBTQ+ people.
- [ ] The institution provides support programs, safe-environment assurances, or community resources 
      for LGBTQ+ individuals.
- [ ] The institution does not engage in policies or actions that disadvantage LGBTQ+ students, staff, 
      patients, or community members.
- [ ] The institution does not undermine, weaken, or remove LGBTQ+ protections.

### B. Financial & Operational Use Requirements
- [ ] Any financial or operational benefit derived from this software is reinvested into:
      - LGBTQ+ protection or support,
      - public-interest health or research work,
      - patient care,
      - community services.
- [ ] No benefit is used for discriminatory, punitive, or anti-LGBTQ purposes.
- [ ] Finance, budgeting, forecasting, or strategy departments meet the same LGBTQ+ protection standards.

### C. Prohibited Uses (Institution Must Verify)
- [ ] No proprietary trading, hedge-fund strategies, alpha modeling, or market-making.
- [ ] No commercial sale, sublicensing, or for-profit deployment.
- [ ] No use that generates profit without LGBTQ+ or public-health reinvestment.
- [ ] No use in ways that harm, surveil, restrict, or disadvantage LGBTQ+ people.

### D. Enforcement & Audit
- [ ] Institution acknowledges automatic termination if conditions are violated.
- [ ] Institution agrees to discontinue use immediately upon violation.
