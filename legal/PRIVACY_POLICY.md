# Fox ML Infrastructure — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fox ML Infrastructure LLC ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you visit our website, use our software, or interact with our services.

## 1. Executive Summary

**Fox ML Infrastructure does not collect, store, or process personal data from users of our software.**

Our software is **client-hosted**, meaning it runs entirely on your infrastructure. We have no access to your data, no ability to collect your data, and no infrastructure that could store your data.

This Privacy Policy covers:
- **Website usage** (if applicable)
- **Software licensing** (no data collection)
- **Consulting engagements** (limited data access, NDA-protected)
- **Support communications** (email only)
