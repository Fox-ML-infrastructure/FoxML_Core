## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 Brand Misuse

**You may NOT:**
- **Claim affiliation** — Use "Fox ML Infrastructure" to imply endorsement, partnership, or affiliation without written authorization
- **Modify or repurpose logos** — Alter, modify, or create derivative works of Fox ML Infrastructure logos or branding
- **Use for endorsement** — Use the Fox ML Infrastructure name to endorse trading performance, financial results, or investment strategies
- **Domain squatting** — Register domains containing "Fox ML Infrastructure" or variations
- **Trademark registration** — Attempt to register "Fox ML Infrastructure" or similar marks

### 3.2 Trading Performance Claims

**You may NOT:**
- Use "Fox ML Infrastructure" to claim or imply specific trading performance
- Use the name to endorse investment strategies or financial products
- Use the name in a way that suggests Fox ML Infrastructure guarantees trading results

**Example (Prohibited):**
> "Fox ML Infrastructure-powered trading system achieved 50% returns" (implies endorsement)

**Example (Permitted):**
> "Our trading system uses Fox ML Infrastructure. Past performance does not guarantee future results." (attribution without endorsement)
