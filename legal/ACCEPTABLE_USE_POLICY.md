# Fox ML Infrastructure — Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines acceptable and prohibited uses of Fox ML Infrastructure software.  
Violation of this policy may result in termination of the commercial license.

---

## 1. Acceptable Use

### 1.1 Permitted Uses

**Fox ML Infrastructure may be used for:**

- **Research and development** — ML research, quantitative research, and model development
- **Internal operations** — Internal business operations, analytics, and decision-making
- **Trading and investment** — Trading, investment analysis, and portfolio management (subject to compliance with applicable laws)
- **Academic research** — Academic research and educational purposes (under AGPL-3.0 for non-commercial use)
- **Integration** — Integration with proprietary systems and workflows
- **Customization** — Customization and modification for internal use

**All uses must comply with the Commercial License Agreement and applicable laws.**

---

## 2. Prohibited Uses

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be used for:**

- **Illegal trading** — Trading activities that violate securities laws, exchange rules, or financial regulations
- **Market manipulation** — Manipulation of markets, prices, or trading volumes
- **Fraud** — Fraudulent activities, misrepresentation, or deceptive practices
- **Money laundering** — Money laundering or other financial crimes
- **Sanctions violations** — Activities that violate economic sanctions or embargoes
- **Any illegal purpose** — Any use that violates applicable laws or regulations

### 2.2 Sanctioned Entities

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be used by:**

- **Sanctioned entities** — Entities subject to economic sanctions or embargoes (e.g., OFAC, UN, EU sanctions)
- **Prohibited countries** — Entities in countries subject to comprehensive sanctions
- **Terrorist organizations** — Entities designated as terrorist organizations
- **Prohibited persons** — Individuals or entities on prohibited persons lists

**See `legal/EXPORT_COMPLIANCE.md` for export control compliance requirements.**

### 2.3 High-Risk Environments

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be used in:**

- **Medical diagnosis** — Medical diagnosis, treatment, or life-critical medical systems
- **Critical infrastructure** — Critical infrastructure systems where failure could cause significant harm
- **Weapons systems** — Weapons systems, defense systems, or military applications
- **Life support systems** — Life support systems or systems where failure could result in death
- **Nuclear systems** — Nuclear systems or nuclear facility control systems
- **Aviation safety** — Aviation safety systems or flight control systems
- **Any high-risk environment** — Any environment where failure could result in death, serious injury, or significant property damage

**See `COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md` Section 4 (Restrictions) for detailed high-risk environment restrictions.**

### 2.4 Exchange Rule Violations

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be used to:**

- **Violate exchange rules** — Violate exchange rules, trading regulations, or market regulations
- **Circumvent restrictions** — Circumvent trading restrictions, position limits, or regulatory requirements
- **Manipulate markets** — Manipulate markets, prices, or trading volumes
- **Front-running** — Engage in front-running or other prohibited trading practices
- **Insider trading** — Use non-public information for trading (if applicable)

**Licensee is responsible for ensuring compliance with all applicable exchange rules and regulations.**

### 2.5 Unauthorized Redistribution

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be:**

- **Redistributed** — Redistributed, sold, or provided to third parties
- **Hosted as SaaS** — Hosted as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) or Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS)
- **Sublicensed** — Sublicensed, rented, or leased to third parties
- **Published** — Published or disclosed publicly (source code or derivatives)
- **Resold** — Resold or transferred to third parties without authorization

**See `COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md` Section 4 (Restrictions) for detailed redistribution restrictions.**

### 2.6 Unauthorized Access

**Fox ML Infrastructure may NOT be used to:**

- **Unauthorized access** — Gain unauthorized access to systems, networks, or data
- **Security breaches** — Breach security measures or access controls
- **Data theft** — Steal, copy, or misappropriate data or intellectual property
- **System disruption** — Disrupt, damage, or interfere with systems or networks
