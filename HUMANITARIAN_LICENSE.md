## Overview

FoxML Core may be used **free of charge** by organizations whose primary mission is the advancement of public good, humanitarian outcomes, or charitable purposes, **provided that all of the following conditions are met**.

This is a **named exception**, not a separate license. It grants permission to use FoxML Core under specific, enforceable conditions without triggering commercial licensing requirements.

The following organizations may qualify for this exception:

- **Registered non-profit organizations** (501(c)(3) or equivalent)
- **Accredited academic or research institutions**
- **Government or public-sector research bodies**

## Conditions

All of the following conditions must be satisfied:

### 1. No Revenue Generation (Direct or Indirect)

The use must **not generate direct or indirect revenue**.

**Indirect revenue includes but is not limited to:**
- Lead generation
- Internal efficiency gains tied to commercial operations
- Investor signaling
- Use in support of revenue-generating activities
- Brand value or marketing benefit for commercial entities
- Competitive advantage in commercial markets

### 2. No Proprietary Embedding

FoxML Core must **not be embedded in a proprietary or commercial product**.

### 3. No Resale or Redistribution

- No resale, sublicensing, or commercial redistribution
- No packaging or repackaging as part of any commercial offering

### 4. No Commercial Optimization

- No use for internal profit optimization, trading, advertising, or customer monetization
- No use in support of revenue-generating business units

### 5. Attribution Required

Proper attribution to **FoxML Core** and **Jennifer Lewis** is required in:
- Documentation
- Publications
- Outputs derived from use of the Software

Attribution format:
> "This work uses FoxML Core (https://github.com/Fox-ML-infrastructure/FoxML_Core), developed by Jennifer Lewis."

## Organizational Isolation Requirement

For companies or organizations that have both commercial and non-commercial activities:

- The humanitarian use must be **organizationally and operationally isolated** from any commercial activities
- Shared infrastructure, shared personnel (in their commercial capacity), or shared data pipelines between commercial and humanitarian units **disqualifies** the use from this exception
- Shell organization structures or corporate subsidiaries claiming independence from their commercial parent do not qualify

---

## Transition to Commercial Use

If an organization later transitions to commercial use, a **commercial license is required at that point**.

Transition includes:
- Generating revenue (direct or indirect) from work using FoxML Core
- Embedding FoxML Core in a commercial product
- Using FoxML Core outputs to support revenue-generating activities
- Any use that no longer satisfies all conditions of this exception

Organizations must notify the maintainer and obtain a commercial license **before** any commercial use begins.

---

## Termination

This exception is **automatically revoked** if:

- Any condition above is violated
- The organization's status changes such that it no longer qualifies as an eligible organization
- The organization transitions to commercial use without obtaining a commercial license

**Upon termination, continued use constitutes unlicensed commercial use** and is subject to the enforcement provisions in `COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md`, including but not limited to copyright infringement claims, retroactive licensing fees, and statutory damages.
